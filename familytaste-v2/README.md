# FamilyTaste v2

FamilyTaste v2 is the next-generation mobile-first wellness app.

## Goals
- Keep the legacy/root app stable until v2 is approved.
- Build v2 in this folder only.
- Optimize for iPhone and Android first.
- Preserve the human body AI impact experience as a signature feature.

## Core Modules
- Food Journal
- AI Photo Food Scan
- Interactive Human Body
- Wellness and Balanced Diet Program
- Food Intelligence and repeat dish stats
- Restaurant Intelligence
- Family Profiles
- Photo Gallery

## Release Rule
Do not replace the root app until v2 is complete, tested, and approved.
